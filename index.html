About Photo Metadata Viewer

Photo Metadata Viewer is a powerful, privacy-focused tool that helps photographers, designers, and anyone working with digital images to analyze and understand the hidden data embedded in their photos.

Unlike other online tools, all processing happens directly in your browser - meaning your images are never uploaded to a server, ensuring complete privacy and security for your sensitive photos.

This tool was built by photographers for photographers, with a focus on providing a clean, intuitive interface to access technical information that's often buried in your image files.

How to Use

1

Upload Your Image

Drag and drop your photo onto the upload area or click "Choose File" to select an image from your device.

2

View Basic Information

Instantly see basic file information including dimensions, file size, and last modified date.

3

Explore EXIF Data

Expand the EXIF section to view detailed camera settings, GPS coordinates (if available), and other technical metadata.

Supported Image Formats

  • JPEG/JPG - Most common format with full EXIF support
  • PNG - Limited metadata but basic properties are extracted
  • HEIC/HEIF - Apple's High Efficiency Image Format
  • TIFF - Professional format with extensive metadata support
  • WebP - Google's modern image format
  • BMP - Basic bitmap format (limited metadata)

Note: The amount of metadata available varies by format. JPEG images typically contain the most comprehensive EXIF data.
